What the app does

Everything a citizen needs
in one secure place

🤳
Face Recognition Login
Live camera with liveness detection. Biometric score matched against DHA records in real-time.
✍️
Document Signing
Sign legal, medical, corporate and government documents with biometric verification. Legally binding with blockchain audit trail.
🏛️
DHA Records
Direct access to your Home Affairs record. Citizenship, alive status, Smart ID card validity — all verified live.

SASSA Grant Status
Check your grant type, monthly amount and next payment date. Full 12-month payment history available.

Scan and attach your Smart ID or green ID book. MRZ barcode extracted and verified against DHA.
